Questions

CSA FAQs

When is pickup / delivery? +
Local pickup is Thursday mornings at the estate in Indiana. Overnight shipping goes out Wednesday evening for Thursday delivery. You choose at signup.
Can I choose which varieties I receive? +
The share is a curated selection based on what's at peak flush that week. If you have a strong preference or allergy concern, note it at signup and we'll do our best to accommodate.
What if I need to skip a week? +
No problem — just let us know by Tuesday and we'll pause your share for that week. Monthly members get a prorated credit. No penalties.
Do you ship outside Indiana? +
Yes — we ship nationwide via overnight carrier with cold packs. Shipping is included in the share price for contiguous US addresses.
How do I sign up? +
Currently we're accepting reservations through our contact page. We'll confirm availability, set your pickup or shipping preference, and invoice you. Full online checkout is coming in Phase 2.
