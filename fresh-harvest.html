Fresh Harvest FAQs

How do I know which variety to choose?

Each has a distinct character. Blue Oyster is the most versatile — mild, clean flavor with a satisfying chew. Golden Oyster brings a delicate nutty sweetness. Black Pearl is the boldest — rich, savory, with dense, meaty flesh. We recommend trying all three.

Can I mix varieties in one order?

Yes — during Phase 2 we'll offer mixed bundles. For now, each variety is ordered individually. Use the Add to Cart button on each product page.

How should I store fresh mushrooms?

Remove from packaging and place in a paper bag in the refrigerator. Avoid plastic — it traps moisture and accelerates spoilage. Best consumed within 5–7 days. Do not wash until just before use.

Do you offer bulk pricing for restaurants?

Yes — visit our Wholesale page for bulk pricing and direct ordering for restaurants and food service.
