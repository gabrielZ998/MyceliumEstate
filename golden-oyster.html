Golden Oyster

Pleurotus citrinopileatus

Bright golden-yellow caps with a delicate, nutty flavor and a tender, almost crisp texture when young. One of our fastest-fruiting varieties — harvested at peak color before the caps begin to flatten and fade.

Why Golden Oyster

Flavor & Texture

A delicate, nutty flavor with a tender bite — milder than darker oyster varieties and a favorite for lighter dishes.

Cultivation Method

One of our fastest-fruiting varieties, fruited on pasteurized straw substrate and harvested at peak color.

Best Uses

Excellent raw or lightly sautéed to preserve color and texture — a striking addition to salads and pasta dishes.

Questions

How fresh are the mushrooms when I receive them?

Every order is harvested within 24 hours of pickup or delivery — we don't hold inventory in cold storage.

Do you ship Golden Oyster mushrooms?

Not yet. Phase 1 is local pickup and local delivery only. Shipping is planned for a future phase.

How should I store them at home?

Keep refrigerated in a paper bag (not plastic) to preserve texture. Best used within 7–10 days.

Can I order in bulk for a restaurant?

Yes — visit our Wholesale page for bulk pricing and ordering details.
