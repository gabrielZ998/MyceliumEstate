Shipping Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We take the freshness of our products seriously. This policy outlines how we handle shipping for all orders from Mycelial Estate.

Fresh Mushroom Orders

Fresh mushrooms are harvested to order and shipped Monday through Wednesday via overnight carrier to ensure Thursday delivery. Orders placed after Wednesday ship the following week. All fresh shipments include insulated packaging and cold packs to maintain temperature during transit.

Grain Spawn & Liquid Culture Orders

Grain spawn and liquid cultures ship Monday through Thursday. These products are stable with proper insulation and ship via 2-day priority service. Cold packs are included during summer months.

Substrate Block Orders

Straw block kits ship Monday through Wednesday via standard ground service. Blocks are hydrated at 65–70% moisture and sealed in breathable filter bags; they are stable for 7–10 days in transit.

Shipping Rates

Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on package weight, dimensions, and destination. We ship to all 50 contiguous United States. We currently do not ship to Alaska, Hawaii, or international addresses.

Local Pickup

Local pickup is available Thursday mornings from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM at our farm in Southern Indiana. Select "Local Pickup" at checkout and we'll confirm your pickup window by email.

Delivery Issues

If your package arrives damaged or shows signs of temperature abuse, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery at hello@mycelialestate.com with photos and your order number. We will assess and arrange a resolution.

CSA Share Shipping

CSA shares ship weekly on Wednesday for Thursday delivery. Shipping is included in the share price for contiguous US addresses. Local pickup CSA members pick up Thursday mornings.
